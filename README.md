# Run4U

Run4U is a project aimed to provide an all-in-one fitness application to individuals who want to stay healthy and active. This includes people of all fitness levels who are looking for motivation, step and distance tracking, and maintain a balanced diet. Users can track their steps, watch workout videos, and join team workouts to stay motivated. The app also features a diet planner and calories calculator to assist users in making healthier food choices.
